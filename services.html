Our Services

Empowering Cancer Patients in Pakistan with Global Expertise, Local Compassion

At Cancer Care Clinic, led by Dr. Saeed ur Rehman (Specialty Doctor in Medical Oncology, James Cook University Hospital, UK), we deliver world-class virtual cancer care stratified to your needs. Our services bridge the gap between advanced international standards and accessible care in Pakistan.

1. Virtual Oncology Consultations

Expert Guidance from Anywhere in Pakistan

  • Comprehensive Diagnosis & Staging:

    Accurate evaluation using imaging, lab results, and clinical history, aligned with global protocols (e.g., TNM staging).

  • Personalized Treatment Plans:

    Tailored strategies for chemotherapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, or radiation, based on the latest evidence and your unique condition.

  • Specialized Focus:

    Expertise in lung, breast, gastroesophageal, liver, pancreatic, colon, and genitourinary cancers.

2. Detailed Medical Reports

Your Roadmap to Local Treatment

  • Actionable Documentation:

    Receive clear, jargon-free reports for your local healthcare team, including:

    • Diagnosis and stage confirmation
    • Recommended treatment protocols (drug regimens, dosages, cycles)
    • Referrals to trusted oncologists/hospitals in Pakistan
  • Global Standards, Local Relevance:

    We adapt international guidelines (e.g., NHS, ESMO) to ensure compatibility with Pakistani healthcare resources.

3. Second Opinions

Clarity in Uncertainty

  • Review your current diagnosis or treatment plan
  • Get answers to critical questions:
    • Is my diagnosis accurate?
    • Are there newer or less toxic treatment options?
    • What clinical trials might I qualify for?

4. Advanced Care Planning

Long-Term Support for You and Your Family

  • Follow-Up & Monitoring:

    Custom schedules for scans, blood tests, and symptom management.

  • Palliative Care Guidance:

    Improve quality of life with pain management and emotional support strategies.

  • Prognosis Education:

    Honest, compassionate discussions about what to expect at every stage.

5. Multidisciplinary Collaboration

Seamless Care at Your Doorstep

We partner with trusted hospitals and specialists across Pakistan to ensure:

  • Smooth transitions from virtual consultation to local treatment
  • Affordable access to chemotherapy, radiation, or surgery
  • Coordination with nutritionists, counsellors, and palliative care teams

Your Fight Is My Fight

I founded Cancer Care Clinic to ensure no Pakistani patient has to compromise on world-class cancer care. Let me guide you with expertise, empathy, and unwavering commitment.
