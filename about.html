About Dr. Saeed ur Rehman

Specialist in Medical Oncology | Compassionate Cancer Care Advocate

Current Position

I currently serve as a Specialty Doctor in Medical Oncology at James Cook University Hospital, Middlesbrough, United Kingdom, a leading NHS trust renowned for its cutting-edge cancer care and research.

Professional Expertise

With over 14 years of dedicated experience in medical oncology, I specialize in diagnosing and treating a wide spectrum of cancers, with a focused interest in:

  • Lung Cancer
  • Breast Cancer
  • Gastroesophageal & Hepatocellular (Liver) Cancers
  • Pancreatic & Colon Cancers
  • Genitourinary Cancers (Prostate, Bladder, Kidney)
  • Gynaecology cancers (Ovarian, Endometrium, cervix)

My practice prioritizes evidence-based, personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs, ensuring dignity and hope at every stage of their journey.

Qualifications & Credentials

  • Specialty Certificate Examination (SCE) in Medical Oncology (UK)
  • Saudi Fellowship in Medical Oncology
  • MRCP(UK)– Royal College of Physicians, UK
  • MRCPI – Royal College of Physicians of Ireland
  • FCPS(Medicine)- College of Physician and Surgeon, Pakistan
  • PMDC registered

Why I Founded Virtual Cancer Care Clinic

While working in the UK's advanced healthcare system, I recognized the urgent need to extend world-class oncology expertise to patients in Pakistan. Through Cancer Care Clinic, I provide virtual consultations to bridge this gap, empowering patients with accurate diagnoses, globally aligned treatment strategies, and compassionate guidance—all from the comfort of their homes.

My Commitment to You

  • No patient is just a case: I believe in holistic care that addresses both physical and emotional well-being.
  • Clarity & Transparency: I simplify complex medical jargon, ensuring you understand your condition and options.
  • Local Support: I collaborate with trusted hospitals and specialists in Pakistan to help you access quality care near your doorstep.

Let's Navigate Your Journey Together

Whether you seek a second opinion, a detailed treatment plan, or guidance on managing advanced cancer, I am here to walk alongside you with expertise and empathy.
